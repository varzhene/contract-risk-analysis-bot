**Project Overview**

This project is an AI-powered contract analysis assistant designed for small and medium business owners to understand complex legal contracts.
The system analyzes uploaded contracts and identifies risky clauses, penalties, termination risks, auto-renewal conditions, and indemnity terms.
It converts complex legal language into simple business explanations and generates risk scores for better decision-making.

**Key Features**

-> Contract upload (PDF, DOCX, TXT)
-> Automatic text extraction
-> Clause detection
-> Risk scoring (Low / Medium / High)
-> Plain-language explanation
-> Risk identification (Penalty, Termination, Indemnity)
-> AI-based contract understanding
-> PDF report generation
->Streamlit web interface

**Technologies Used**

-- Python
-- Streamlit
-- NLP & Regex Processing
-- PyPDF2
-- python-docx
-- ReportLab

**How It Works**

+ User uploads a contract
+ System extracts text
+ Clauses are detected
+ Risk keywords are analyzed
+ Risk score is calculated
+ Explanations are generated
+ Summary PDF is produced
